2019 American Cancer Society Breast Cancer

Summary: In a cartographic analysis from 2019, the American Cancer Society (ACS) charted the incidence of breast cancer and corresponding fatality rates. The visual representation highlighted notable discrepancies in breast cancer mortality rates, particularly between Black and White women. A subsequent cartographic portrayal in a January 2021 article by the Breast Cancer Research Foundation depicted comparable rates of new breast cancer cases among White and Black women.
