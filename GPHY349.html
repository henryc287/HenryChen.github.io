GPHY349 - GIScience and Public Health

The Identification of Spatial Dependence and Clustering In Our Health Outcome Data Such As Stokes And Visually Explore

Summary: The goal is to determine how stroke episodes are scattered around the city and within each area in. Sheffield, England, using a popular 1990's dataset, and if there is a link with external variables such as Nitrous Oxide (NOx). By visually mapping stroke episodes and statistically assessing whether they are grouped, a possibly hidden cause of increased strokes can be discovered.
