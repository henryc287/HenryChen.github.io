2019 American Cancer Society Breast Cancer

Summary: In a cartographic analysis from 2019, the American Cancer Society (ACS) charted the incidence of
breast cancer and corresponding fatality rates. The visual representation highlighted notable discrepancies in breast cancer
mortality rates, particularly between Black and White women. A subsequent cartographic portrayal in a January 2021 article by
the Breast Cancer Research Foundation depicted comparable rates of new breast cancer cases among White and Black women.

Kingston, Ontario

The Identification of Spatial Dependence and Clustering In Our Health Outcome Data Such As Stokes And Visually Explore
Summary: The goal is to determine how stroke episodes are scattered around the city and within each area in
Sheffield, England, using a popular 1990's dataset, and if there is a link with external variables such as Nitrous Oxide
(NOx). By visually mapping stroke episodes and statistically assessing whether they are grouped, a possibly hidden cause of
increased strokes can be discovered.
